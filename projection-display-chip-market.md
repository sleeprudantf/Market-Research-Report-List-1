<p><p>The projection display chip market primarily consists of two main types: CMOS chips and DMD chips. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) chips are known for their low power consumption, high integration, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for compact displays in smartphones and projectors. DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) chips utilize micro mirrors for precise light modulation, delivering superior image quality and performance in larger projection systems. Both technologies are essential in enhancing visual experiences across various applications, from entertainment to business presentations.</p></p>
