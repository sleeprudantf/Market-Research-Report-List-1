<p><p>The automobile lubricating oil market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions. Hydrogenated lubricants, produced through hydrogenation processes, offer enhanced stability and higher viscosity indices, making them suitable for modern engines. Isomerization dewaxing lubricants involve altering the structure of base oils to improve low-temperature flow properties, resulting in superior performance in cold climates. Together, these types ensure optimal engine efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
