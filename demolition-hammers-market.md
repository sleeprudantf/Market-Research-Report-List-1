<p><p>Demolition hammers are categorized into three main types: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic. Electric demolition hammers are powered by electricity and are ideal for smaller tasks in indoor settings, offering a balance of power and portability. Hydraulic hammers utilize pressurized fluid to deliver high impact energy, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks in construction and demolition projects. Pneumatic hammers operate using compressed air, providing versatility and high performance, often used in industrial environments and roadwork. Each type serves distinct applications based on power sources and operational efficiency.</p></p>
