<p><p>The thermoforming films market is primarily divided into rigid and flexible categories. Rigid thermoforming films are solid, offering strength and durability, making them ideal for packaging applications requiring structural integrity, such as trays and containers. Flexible thermoforming films, on the other hand, are pliable and can adapt to various shapes, providing excellent versatility for applications like blisters and pouches. Both types cater to diverse industries, including food packaging, medical, and consumer goods, driven by innovation and sustainability trends.</p></p>
