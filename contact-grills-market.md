<p><strong>The Contact Grills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Propane (LP) Grills</li><li>Natural Gas (NG) Grills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contact grills market primarily includes two types: Liquid Propane (LP) grills and Natural Gas (NG) grills. LP grills utilize liquid propane as fuel, offering portability and flexibility, making them ideal for outdoor use or areas without natural gas lines. Conversely, NG grills are connected to existing natural gas lines, providing a constant fuel source, which is more convenient for stationary setups. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for convenience, cooking style, and location, driving market demand.</p></p>
