<p><p>The train wheel safety sensor market is primarily divided into two types: single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors monitor the condition of individual wheels, providing critical data on issues like wear and tear, misalignment, or temperature fluctuations. In contrast, double wheel sensors are designed to assess the performance and safety of paired wheels, offering a more comprehensive view of wheel performance and stability. Both types enhance railway safety and efficiency by enabling timely maintenance and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
