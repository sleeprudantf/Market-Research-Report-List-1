<p><strong>The Residential Dehumidifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical/ Refrigerative Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential dehumidifiers market primarily features two types: mechanical (refrigerative) and desiccant dehumidifiers. Mechanical dehumidifiers use refrigeration technology to cool air, condensing moisture, making them effective in warmer environments. They are energy-efficient for larger spaces. In contrast, desiccant dehumidifiers employ hygroscopic materials to absorb moisture from the air, functioning well in colder conditions and low humidity areas. Each type serves different environmental needs, catering to varying consumer preferences for humidity control in residential settings.</p></p>
