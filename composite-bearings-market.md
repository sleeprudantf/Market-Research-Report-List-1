<p><strong>The Composite Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Matrix</li><li>Metal Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite bearings market can be categorized into two main types: fiber matrix and metal matrix. Fiber matrix bearings are made from polymer reinforced with fibers, providing high strength-to-weight ratios and excellent wear resistance, suitable for lightweight applications. In contrast, metal matrix bearings combine metals with reinforcing materials, offering enhanced load-carrying capacity and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for heavy-duty and high-temperature environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, addressing specific performance requirements.</p></p>
