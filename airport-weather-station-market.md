<p><p>The airport weather station market is divided into two primary types: embedded and portable. Embedded airport weather stations are permanently installed within airport facilities, offering continuous, real-time data collection on weather conditions crucial for flight operations and safety. In contrast, portable airport weather stations are mobile units that can be easily transported to different locations, providing flexibility and immediate weather monitoring for temporary operations or remote airfields. Both types play essential roles in enhancing aviation safety and efficiency.</p></p>
