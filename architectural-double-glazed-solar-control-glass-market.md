<p><p>The architectural double glazed solar control glass market features two primary types: absorbing and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is designed to absorb solar radiation, reducing heat transfer while allowing natural light to enter. Reflective solar control glass, on the other hand, uses a reflective coating to bounce back solar energy, minimizing heat gain and glare. Both types enhance energy efficiency in buildings, providing comfort and reducing reliance on air conditioning, while contributing to sustainable architecture.</p></p>
