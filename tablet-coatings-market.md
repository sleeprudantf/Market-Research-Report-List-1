<p><p>Tablet coatings enhance the functionality and appearance of tablets. Icing provides a pleasant finish, while half film coats offer straightforward protection without altering drug release. Enteric coatings prevent dissolution in the stomach, targeting drug release in the intestines. Slow release garments are designed for gradual drug delivery over time, ensuring prolonged therapeutic effects. Controlled release garments further regulate release timings, allowing tailored dosing schedules. Together, these coatings improve patient compliance and drug efficacy.</p></p>
