<p><strong>The Artificial Hip Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Hip Replacement</li><li>Partial Hip Replacement</li><li>Revision Surgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial hip prosthesis market primarily encompasses three types: total hip replacement, partial hip replacement, and revision surgery. Total hip replacement involves replacing the entire hip joint with a prosthetic device, addressing severe arthritis or damage. Partial hip replacement focuses on replacing only the femoral head, often used in specific fractures. Revision surgery is conducted to replace or repair previously implanted hip prosthetics, typically due to wear or complications. Each type serves distinct orthopedic needs, driving market demand and innovation.</p></p>
