<p><p>The soft optical glass market is segmented into single coated, double coated, and triple coated glass types. Single coated glass features a single layer of anti-reflective coating, enhancing light transmission. Double coated glass has two layers, providing improved anti-reflection benefits and greater durability. Triple coated glass offers the highest performance, combining three layers for maximum light transmission, minimized glare, and superior scratch resistance. Each type caters to varying optical needs across applications such as photography, telescopes, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
