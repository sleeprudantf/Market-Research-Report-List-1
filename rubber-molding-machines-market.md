<p><p>Rubber molding machines are essential for shaping rubber into various products through different techniques. Injection molding machines inject molten rubber into molds under pressure, ensuring precise and complex designs. Compression molding machines involve placing rubber in a heated mold, where heat and pressure shape the material, ideal for simpler parts. Other types include transfer molding, which combines features of both methods, allowing for flexibility in production. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse industries and product requirements.</p></p>
