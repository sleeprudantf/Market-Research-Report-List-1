<p><p>The small molecule targeted cancer therapy market includes various types, such as monoclonal antibodies, small molecules, and small molecule drug conjugates. Monoclonal antibodies are lab-engineered proteins that target specific antigens on cancer cells, facilitating immune response. Small molecules are low molecular weight compounds that can penetrate cells, interfering with cancer cell function. Small molecule drug conjugates combine a cytotoxic drug with a targeting molecule, delivering treatment directly to cancer cells while minimizing damage to healthy tissues. Each type enhances precision in cancer treatment.</p></p>
