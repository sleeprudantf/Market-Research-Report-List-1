<p><strong>The Automatic Self-checkout Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescence detection</li><li>Magnetic detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic self-checkout counter market includes two main detection types: fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection utilizes light to identify items, enhancing accuracy in recognizing products by analyzing light emission from barcodes or packaging. In contrast, magnetic detection relies on the magnetic properties of security tags, alerting the system when unauthorized items are scanned. Both technologies improve efficiency and reduce theft, catering to various retail environments seeking streamlined operations and enhanced inventory control.</p></p>
