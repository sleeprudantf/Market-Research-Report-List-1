<p><p>Zinc oxide pigment is primarily available in two forms: powder and dust. The powder form is finely milled, offering excellent coverage and dispersibility, making it ideal for applications in paints, coatings, and plastics. The dust form, characterized by a lighter texture, is often used in applications requiring thinner layering, such as cosmetics and personal care products. Both types provide ultraviolet protection and enhance durability, catering to diverse industries that prioritize performance, safety, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
