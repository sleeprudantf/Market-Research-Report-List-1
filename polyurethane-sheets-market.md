<p><p>The polyurethane sheets market is divided into flexible and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials offer elasticity and high tear strength, making them ideal for applications like cushioning, seals, and gaskets. They are lightweight and versatile, suitable for various industries, including automotive, textiles, and medical. In contrast, rigid urethane materials provide superior structural support and durability, commonly used in construction, insulation, and automotive components. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing performance, cost, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
