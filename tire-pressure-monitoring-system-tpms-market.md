<p><p>Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is a safety feature in vehicles that alerts drivers when tire pressure is too low, enhancing road safety and fuel efficiency. The market for TPMS is segmented into two main types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors mounted in each tire to measure pressure directly and provide real-time data. Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, estimates tire pressure through wheel speed sensors, detecting variations based on tire rotation and performance.</p></p>
