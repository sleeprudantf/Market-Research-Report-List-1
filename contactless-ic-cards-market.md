<p><p>Contactless IC cards are categorized primarily into memory cards and microprocessor cards. Memory cards store data without processing capabilities, making them suitable for simple applications like access control and ticketing. In contrast, microprocessor cards feature an embedded chip that can process data, enabling advanced functions such as secure transactions and multi-application usage. The microprocessor cards offer enhanced security and versatility, making them ideal for banking, identity verification, and mobile payments, thus catering to a broader range of market needs.</p></p>
