<p><p>The panel photoresist stripper market is segmented into solvent and semi-aqueous types. Solvent strippers utilize organic solvents to dissolve and remove photoresist, providing effective results but raising concerns about environmental and health hazards. In contrast, semi-aqueous strippers combine both solvents and water, offering a milder approach that minimizes hazardous waste and promotes safer handling. This dual strategy caters to the diverse preferences of semiconductor manufacturers aiming for efficiency and sustainability in the photolithography process during circuit fabrication.</p></p>
