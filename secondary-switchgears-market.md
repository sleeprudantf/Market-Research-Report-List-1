<p><strong>The Secondary Switchgears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-insulated</li><li>Vacuum-insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary switchgears market is primarily categorized into gas-insulated and vacuum-insulated technologies. Gas-insulated switchgears (GIS) utilize sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation and arc extinction, offering compact designs and high reliability in urban environments. They are ideal for applications requiring space efficiency. Conversely, vacuum-insulated switchgears operate by interrupting electrical arcs in a vacuum, providing enhanced safety and durability. These technologies cater to different operational requirements, with GIS favored for high voltage applications and vacuum-insulated systems for lower voltage scenarios.</p></p>
