<p><p>The Serum Amyloid A Test Kit market is categorized into two main types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent markers and time-resolved detection for high sensitivity and specificity, making it suitable for precise measurements of serum amyloid A levels. On the other hand, CLIA employs a chemiluminescent output, providing rapid results with enhanced sensitivity, ideal for clinical diagnostics in various settings. Both methods are essential in monitoring inflammatory conditions and amyloidosis.</p></p>
