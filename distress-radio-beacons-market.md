<p><p>Distress Radio Beacons encompass three key market types: Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs), Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacons (EPIRBs), and Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs). PLBs are portable devices used by individuals for emergency signaling in remote areas, while EPIRBs are designed for maritime use, activating automatically upon immersion in water to alert authorities. ELTs are commonly used in aviation, transmitting distress signals when an aircraft crashes. Each type serves a crucial role in enhancing safety and facilitating rescue operations.</p></p>
