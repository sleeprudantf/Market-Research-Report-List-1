<p><p>The automotive plastics market comprises various types, each with unique properties. Polycarbonate (PC) is strong and transparent, suitable for safety applications. Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) is transparent and shatter-resistant. Polyamide (PA), or nylon, offers high strength and thermal resistance. Other materials include engineering plastics and specialty blends.</p></p>
