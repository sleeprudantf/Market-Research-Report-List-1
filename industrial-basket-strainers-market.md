<p><p>The Industrial Basket Strainers market primarily consists of two types: Simplex Basket Strainers and Duplex Basket Strainers. Simplex Basket Strainers are single-filter systems designed for straightforward applications, permitting easy maintenance and cleaning while the system is offline. Duplex Basket Strainers, on the other hand, feature dual-filter configurations that allow for continuous operation, enabling one strainer to be serviced while the other remains in use. Both types are essential for protecting equipment from debris and maintaining optimal fluid flow in various industrial processes.</p></p>
