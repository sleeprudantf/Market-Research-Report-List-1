<p><p>The adhesives market comprises various types based on their curing mechanisms and applications. Pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) bond on contact and are removable, ideal for tapes and labels. Reactive adhesives cure through chemical reactions, offering strong bonds suitable for structural applications. Drying adhesives rely on solvent evaporation or moisture removal for bonding, often used in paints and coatings. Other categories may include hot melt adhesives, which solidify upon cooling, and specialty adhesives tailored for specific materials or conditions.</p></p>
